Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb Squash
  • 2 pieces Taro
  • 4 thumbs Ginger
  • 2 stalks Lemongrass
  • 1 piece Eggplant
  • 10 pieces String Beans
  • 10 pieces Okra
  • 3 1/2 Water
  • Salt and Ground Pepper

Procedures

  1. Boil water in a pot. Add lemongrass and ginger. Cook for 5 mins.
  2. Add taro and squash, cover and continue boiling for 7 mins.
  3. Add okra, string beans, and eggplant. Stir. Cover and continue cooking.
  4. Season with salt and ground pepper.
  5. Transfer to serving bowl. Enjoy!
