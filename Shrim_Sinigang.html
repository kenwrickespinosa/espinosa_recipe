Shrimp Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Shrimp
  • 1 bunch Kangkong
  • 15 pieces Snake Beans
  • 5 pieces Okra
  • 1 piece Eggplant
  • 1 cup Daikon Radish
  • 1 piece Tomato
  • 1 piece Onion
  • 2 quarts water
  • Fish Sauce and Ground Black Pepper

Procedures

  1. Boil water and a cooking oil. Add onion, tomato, and radish. Cover and continue to boil for 8 mins.
  2. Add shrimp. Cook for 2 mins.
  3. Add snake beans, okra, and eggplant. Stir. Cook for 5 mins.
  4. Put the kangkong stalks into the pot. Add fish sauce, salt, and ground black pepper.
  5. Add kangkong leaves. Cook for 1 minutes.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl and enjoy!
