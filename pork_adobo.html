Pork Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons Oil
  • 2 pounds Pork
  • 1/4 cup Vinegar
  • 1/3 cup Soy Sauce
  • 6 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 teaspoons Sugar
  • 2 cups Water

Procedures

  1. Heat a pot, add oil and sear the pork until browned on all sides.
  2. Add the vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and peppercorn.
  3. Mix the Sugar and water, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium low, cover and simmer for 1 hour.
  4. Remove the cover and continue simmering for another 30 mins to reduce sauce.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
