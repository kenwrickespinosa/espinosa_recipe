Chicken Afritada

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Chicken
  • 2 pieces Potato
  • 1 piece Carrot
  • 8 oz. Tomato Sauce
  • 3 pieces Hotdog
  • 1/2 Green Peas
  • 3 tablespoons Cooking Oil
  • 3 loaves of garlic and onion
  • Salt and Black Pepper

Procedures

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a pot. Saute the Onion and Garlic until the onion softens.
  2. Add chicken. Cook for 30 seconds. Turn it over and cook the other side for 30 seconds.
  3. Pour tomato sauce, cover, and let it boil.
  4. Add hotdogs and carrots, cook for few minutes.
  5. Add potato, cover the pot and cook for 8 minutes.
  6. Add green peas.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, then serve!
