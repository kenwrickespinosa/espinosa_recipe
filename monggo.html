Monggo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Munggo Beans
  • 6 ounces Pork
  • 1 cup Malunggay Leaves
  • 2 medium Tomato
  • 2 medium Onion
  • 1/2 cup Chicharon
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons Cooking Oil

Procedures

  1. Heat oil in cooking pot.
  2. Saute garlic, onion, and tomato.
  3. Add the pork. Cook until the color of the pork turns light brown.
  4. Pour water.
  5. Add the munggo beans. Cover and adjust heat between low to medium.
  6. Put malunggay leaves, stir.
  7. Add ground black pepper and cook for 5 mins.
  8. Transfer to a serving bowl and enjoy!
